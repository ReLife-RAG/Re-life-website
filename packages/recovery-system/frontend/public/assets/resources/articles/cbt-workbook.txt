The CBT Workbook for Recovery

Welcome to your practical CBT workbook.

1. Identify Trigger
Write one trigger that challenged your recovery today.

2. Thought Check
What automatic thought appeared?
Is it fully true, partially true, or distorted?

3. Reframe
Replace that thought with a balanced statement that supports healing.

4. Action Step
Choose one small action for the next 15 minutes.

Repeat daily to build emotional resilience and relapse prevention habits.
